Observations – Conflicting Metrics

  • Accuracy is misleading due to class imbalance – always predicting "legitimate" gets ~98%.
  • Precision vs Recall: Logistic Regression catches more fraud but with many false alarms.
  • F1-Score balances this – the tuned threshold model achieves the best F1.
  • ROC-AUC is threshold-independent and confirms the improved RF generalises best.
